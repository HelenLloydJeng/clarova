AI Training Programmes

Clarova provides practical, public-sector-ready AI training for executives, governance leads, and communications teams. Our goal is to help you lead, manage, and communicate AI adoption with confidence.

Why It Matters

AI adoption is already underway in most public sector organisations — often without strategic oversight. Tools like ChatGPT, Copilot, and AI-assisted platforms are being used daily by staff, often informally and without training, policies, or safeguards.

Our strategic training is designed to help executive leaders, digital governance leads, and communications professionals understand the risks and opportunities of AI, and implement a clear, responsible response framework. This isn’t just about tools — it’s about trust, accountability, and leading change with clarity.

Clarova equips public sector leaders with the situational awareness, governance models, and communications strategy needed to lead AI adoption responsibly and credibly — before policy catches up or crisis hits.

Strategic AI Training for Leaders

Built for public sector executives, SLTs, digital leads and governance professionals. This training supports informed decision-making, policy setting, and accountability in AI use.

  • Executive Briefings – Strategic 60-min sessions for senior leaders or boards
  • AI Governance Workshops – Half-day or full-day sessions on risk, policy, and implementation frameworks
  • Webinars – Topical sessions addressing emerging AI risks or regulation changes

AI Training for Communications Teams

Comms professionals are already using tools like ChatGPT — often without clear guidance or policy. Our comms-focused training gives teams the confidence, controls, and creative frameworks to use AI responsibly in high-stakes environments.

  • Scenario-Based Workshops – Crisis simulations and real-world AI use in stakeholder messaging
  • Hands-On Clinics – Practical sessions using generative tools under supervision
  • Policy & Risk Awareness – Understand platform terms, data risks, and reputational safeguards

Teams leave with the tools to use AI safely and credibly — without compromising public trust or reputation.

This training can be delivered via webinar or in-house and aligns with NHS and government communications standards.
